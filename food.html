Korean Fried Chicken

Korean Fried Chicken (깐풍기) is a boneless crispy chicken that is deep-fried with starch and marinated with chili oil and garlic, and ginger. It is get stirred with spicy sauce along with several types of vegetables.

Origin

The idea was inspired by Kung Pao chicken, classic dish in Sichuan cuisine originated in the Sichuan Province of south-western China

Ingredients (3 to 4 serving)
  • Chicken leg 300g
  • Leek 30g
  • Onions 25g
  • Water 3 teaspoon
  • Chili Oil, Soy, Vingar, Sugar 1~2 teaspoon
